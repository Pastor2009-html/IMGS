Admissions

Admission Process

We welcome applications for new students. Our admission process is designed to ensure the best fit for our school community.

Requirements:

  • Age appropriate for primary level (3-11 years)
  • Completed previous grade or readiness assessment
  • Basic health records

Process:

  1. Submit application form online or in person
  2. Entrance assessment and interview
  3. Parent/guardian meeting
  4. Acceptance notification
