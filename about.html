About Innoglad Montessori Global School

Our primary school has been serving the community for over 15 years, committed to excellence in education for young minds.

Mission: To provide a nurturing environment where every child can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally through innovative and engaging learning experiences.

Vision: To Raise Leaders That Would Be God Fearing, Discipline, Cultured, Integrity, Team Spirit, Accountable, Humble, Confident, And Academy Excellence To Affect The World Positively.

Values: Excellence, Integrity, Creativity, Compassion, and Community.
